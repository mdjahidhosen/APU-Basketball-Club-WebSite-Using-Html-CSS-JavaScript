Our Goal

APU Basketball Club's goal is to promote and provide basketball programmes and services that stimulate participants' health, development, and enjoyment in a safe environment while also developing fitness, wellness, social interaction, teamwork, fair play, representative, and leadership opportunities.To develop the APU Basketball Club as a forward-thinking basketball organisation by offering opportunities for all skill levels of players, coaches, and officials in a welcoming family environment.
